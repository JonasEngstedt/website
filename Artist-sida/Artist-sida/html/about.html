Me:

I'm a 19 year old kid with a passion for music, I've been playing music for as long as I remember and would love to one day be able to share my music with the whole world.

I mainly play guitar and piano, but I have basic knowledge of the most common instruments for example bass, drums and ukulele.

In each and everyone of my songs I play and produce everything on my own, my main goal is to keep producing every instrument on my own but would like to someday team up with someone and create a collaboration.
